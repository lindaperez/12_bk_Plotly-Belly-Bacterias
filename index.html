Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard

Dashboard to display a panel for demographic information and visualize the bacterial data for each volunteer subject ID. Every volunteer or scientist is able to identify per sample/subject the top 10 bacterial species in their belly buttons the whasing frequecy and the Bacteria Culture.

Instructions: To display the interactive charts click from the dropdown the Test Subject ID in the left navbar.

The chart above shows 10 baterias species with the hightest sample values, if you hover the bars you can see the culture category.

The gauge chart above shows the washing frequecy of every subject. To determine if there is a relations with the meet or it is just an external factor.

The bubble chart shows the severity of every subject by the size of the bubble. The bigger bubbles has the bigger sample values. It shows a general perspective of the distribution of all the samples then it could be helpful understand how is the selected subject comparing it wit chart of Bateria cultures.
